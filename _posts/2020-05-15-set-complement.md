Complement of set $A$ (denoted as $\bar{A}$) will be the [Set Difference]({% post_url 2020-05-14-set-difference %}) $U-A$. Where $U$ is [Universal Set]({% post_url 2020-05-12-universal-set %}).

$$\bar{A} = \{ x | (x \in U) \wedge (x \notin A)\}$$
