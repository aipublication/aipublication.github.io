For proposition $p \to q$, *inverse* will be $\neg p \to \neg q$.

Inverse of a proposition is [Equivalent]({% post_url 2020-01-12-equivalent %}) to [Converse]({% post_url 2020-01-11-converse %}) of the proposition.
