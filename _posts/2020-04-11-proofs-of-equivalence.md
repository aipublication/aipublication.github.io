To prove a [Biconditional]({% post_url 2020-01-16-biconditional %}) statement $p \leftrightarrow  q$ we have to prove that both statements $p \to q$ and $q \to p$ are true.
$$p \leftrightarrow q \to (p \to q ) \wedge (q \to p)$$
