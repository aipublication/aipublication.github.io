If n=2, then [Ordered n-tuples]({% post_url 2020-05-06-ordered-n-tuples %}) are called ordered pairs.
Two ordered pairs $a_i$ and $b_i$ are equal if and only if
$$(a_1, a_2, \dots a_n) = (b_1, b_2, \dots b_n)\ for\ i=1,2, \dots n$$
