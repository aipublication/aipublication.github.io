Join of two zero-one matrices $A$ and $B$ (denoted as $A \vee B$) is the [Disjunction]({% post_url 2020-01-08-disjunction %}) of their entries.


































































































































































































































































































































































$$A \vee B = [a_{ij} \vee b_{ij}]$$
