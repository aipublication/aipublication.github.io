Union of two [Sets]({% post_url 2021-06-08-sets %}) $A$ and $B$ is the set that contains elements from both sets.
























































































































































































































































































































































































































It is denoted as $A \cup B$.

























































































































































































































































































































































































































$$A \cup B = \{ x | (x \in A) \vee (x \in B)\}$$
