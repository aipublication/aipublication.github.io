Given $a$ and $b$ are positive integers with $a \ge b$. Then number of divisions done by [[Euclidean Algorithm]] to find [[Greatest Common Divisor (GCD)]] of $a$ and $b$ is $5\ \times$ the number of decimal digits in $b$.
