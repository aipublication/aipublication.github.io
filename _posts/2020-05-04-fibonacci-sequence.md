Named after Italian Mathematician [[Fibonacci]].

It is a [[Recurrance Relations]] defined by [[Initial Condition]] ($f_0 = 0$ and $f_1 = 1$).

$$f_n = f_{(n-1)} + f_{(n-2)}$$ for $n = 2, 3, \dots$
