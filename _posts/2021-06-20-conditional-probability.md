Let $E$ and $F$ be two events such that outcome of $E$ depends on outcome of $F$ and $p(F)>0$ then conditional probability ($p(E | F)$) is


































































































































































































































































































































































$$p(E|F) = {p(E \cap F) \over p(F)}$$
