Also known as *Law of Detachment*.

It states that if a [Conditional Statement]({% post_url 2021-06-03-conditional-statement %}) and its <b>Hypothesis</b> is true then the <b>Conclusion</b> must also be true.

$$(p \wedge (p \to q)) \to q$$
