Let *p* be a [Proposition]({% post_url 2020-01-01-proposition %}).
Then *negation* of p will be denoted by $\neg p$.


































































































































































































































































































































































[Truth Value]({% post_url 2020-01-03-truth-value %}) of $\neg P$ will be opposite of *p*.
