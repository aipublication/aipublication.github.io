Sequences of form $a_1, a_2, \dots a_n$ or $a_1 a_2 a_3 \dots a_n$ are called *Strings* in computer science. Similar to [Bit String]({% post_url 2020-01-21-bit-string %}).

An *empty string* is represented as $\lambda$.
