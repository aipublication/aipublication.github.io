When probability is expressed as a function $p()$ over outcomes $x_1, x_2, \dots x_n$ such that

































































































































































































































































































































































$$0 \le p(x_i) \le 1$$ for $i=1, 2, \dots n$

































































































































































































































































































































































and
$$\sum_{i=1}^n p(x_i) = 1$$
