[[Cardinality]] of union of two sets will be sum of cardinality of both sets less the cardinality of their [[Set Intersection]].

$$|A \cup B| = |A| + |B| - |A \cap B|$$

Generalized form of this is called *Principle of Inclusion-Exclusion*.
