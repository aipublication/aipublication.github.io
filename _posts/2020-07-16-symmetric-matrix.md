A square matrix is *symmetric* if it is equal to [Transpose of Matrices]({% post_url 2020-07-15-transpose-of-matrices %}) i.e. $A^T = A$.

































































































































































































































































































































































$A = [a_{ij}]$ is symmetric if $a_{ij} = a_{ji}$.
