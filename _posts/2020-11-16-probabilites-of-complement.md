Let $E$ be an [Event]({% post_url 2020-11-15-event %}) in sample space [Sample Space]({% post_url 2020-11-14-sample-space %}) $S$ then probability of [Set Complement]({% post_url 2020-05-15-set-complement %}) of $E$ ($\bar{E}$) is
$$p(\bar{E}) = 1 - p(E)$$
