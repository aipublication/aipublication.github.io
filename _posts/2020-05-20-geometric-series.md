Sum of terms of [[Geometric Progression (GP)]].

Suppose in a GP $a$ and $r$ are real numbers and $r \neq 0$
$$\sum_{j=0}^{n} ar^{j} = 
\begin{cases} 
      {ar^{n+1}-a \over r-1} & if\ r \neq 1 \\
      (n+1)a & if\ r = 1
   \end{cases}
$$
