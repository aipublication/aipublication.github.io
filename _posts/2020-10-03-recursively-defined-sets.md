A [[Sets]] $\Sigma^*$ over [[Alphabet]] $\Sigma$ is defined recursively by
**Basis Step**: $\lambda \in \Sigma$ (Where $\lambda$ is an [[Empty String]])
**Recursive Step**: If $w \in \Sigma^*$ and $x \in \Sigma^*$ then $wx \in \Sigma^*$
