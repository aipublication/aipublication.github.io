A recurrance [Relation]({% post_url 2020-05-09-relation %}) expresses a term $\{ a_n \}$ in terms of one or more its previous terms $\{ a_0, a_1, a_2, \dots a_{(n-1)} \}$ for all integer $n$. Such that $n \ge n_0$. Here $n_0$ is a non-negative integer.
