When [[Composistion of Functions]] of [[Inverse of Functions]] and the function itself is taken, it creates an *identity function*.

$$(f^{-1} \circ f) (a) = f^{-1}(f(a)) = a = \iota_{a}$$
$$(f \circ f^{-1}) (b) = f(f^{-1}(b)) = b = \iota_{b}$$
