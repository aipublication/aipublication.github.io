[Existential Quantifier]({% post_url 2020-02-23-existential-quantifier %})
It concludes that $P(c)$ is true for some $c$ that is part of the domain. Given $\exists xP(x)$ is true.

$\exists xP(x)$
$\therefore P(c)\ for\ some\ element\ c$
