[[Event]]s $E_1, E_2, \dots E_n$ are mutually independent if $p(E_{i_1} \cap E_{i_2} \dots \cap E_{i_m}) = p(E_{i_1})p(E_{i_2}) \dots p(E_{i_m})$ for $i_j, j = 1, 2, \dots m$ for $1 \le i_1 < i_2 \dots < i_m \le n$ and $m \ge 2$.
