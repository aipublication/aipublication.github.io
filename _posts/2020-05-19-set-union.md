Union of two [Sets]({% post_url 2020-07-03-sets %}) $A$ and $B$ is the set that contains elements from both sets.

































































































































































































































































































































































It is denoted as $A \cup B$.


































































































































































































































































































































































$$A \cup B = \{ x | (x \in A) \vee (x \in B)\}$$
