Also known as *Law of Detachment*.

It states that if a [Conditional Statement]({% post_url 2020-01-15-conditional-statement %}) and its [[Hypothesis]] is true then the [[Conclusion]] must also be true.

$$(p \wedge (p \to q)) \to q$$
