[[Disjunction]] or [[Negation]] of [[Proposition]] variables.

Example:
$$p \vee (q \wedge r) \equiv (p \vee q) \wedge (p \vee r)$$
Both $(p \vee q)$ and $(p \vee r)$ are Clauses.
So $p \vee (q \wedge r)$ could be expressed as two clauses.
