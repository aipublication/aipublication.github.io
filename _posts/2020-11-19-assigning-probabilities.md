Let $S$ be a sample space with finite number of outcomes then probability of each outcome $s$ ($p(s)$) is given as

































































































































































































































































































































































$$0 \le p(s) \le 1$$

































































































































































































































































































































































and $$\sum_{s \in S} p(s) = 1$$
