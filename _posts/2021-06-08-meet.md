Meet of two zero-one matrices $A$ and $B$ (denoted as $A \wedge B$) is the [Conjunction]({% post_url 2021-06-03-conjunction %}) of their entries.

































































































































































































































































































































































































$$A \wedge B = [a_{ij} \wedge b_{ij}]$$
