[Universal Quantifier]({% post_url 2020-02-22-universal-quantifier %})
It concludes that $\forall xP(x)$ is true given that $P(c)$ is true for all elements $c$ in domain.


































































































































































































































































































































































$P(c)\ for\ an\ arbitrary\ c$

































































































































































































































































































































































$\therefore \forall xP(x)$
